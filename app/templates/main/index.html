Our Story

Where the farm
meets the future

Caffeine & Chaos was born from the belief that Kenya's finest coffee deserved a stage worthy of its complexity. While our roots remain deeply connected to the dedicated growers of Uasin Gishu, our mission has evolved into bringing the complete coffee experience directly to you.

We curate and sell premium specialty coffee bags and essential brewing gear, designed for those who treat coffee as more than just a morning routine. We're not just a brand. We're a movement. A community. A culture built around the ritual of the cup and the stories behind every bean.

The Culture

More than coffee.
It's a community.

Caffeine & Chaos is more than a destination; it’s where a shared passion for the craft comes to life. We believe that every cup is an invitation to connect, and every gathering is an opportunity to celebrate the artistry behind the brew. We provide the essentials for a culture that values quality and conversation. Whether you’re a seasoned barista or a home enthusiast, you aren't just buying coffee—you're joining a collective of rebels and dreamers who find beauty in the chaos and clarity in the cup. Every sip starts a story; every memory starts here.
